## Tips for Your Email

### Before You Send:
- [ ] Read through .github/AI-WORKFLOW.md one more time
- [ ] Prepare 60-second answer on AI usage (practice it)
- [ ] Complete DEPLOYMENT.md and get live URL
- [ ] Test login: admin@acme.com / password123
- [ ] Record quick 5-7 minute video demo

### After You Send:
- [ ] Send follow-up in 2-3 hours with live URLs
- [ ] Include: Live app URL + Video demo link
- [ ] Keep it brief: "Live demos are now available at..."
- [ ] Available for questions immediately

### Tone Notes:
- Professional but warm (you want to work with these people)
- Confident in your work (you should be)
- Humble but not self-deprecating
- Reference the docs (show you've documented thinking)
- Mention AI thoughtfully (not as excuse, as strategy)
